Crystal Collector

How does this work?

  • Check your target score. The numbers you gain from each crystal will amount towards the target score. This will change each round.
  • Tap any of the four crystals. Each has a hidden value, so you'll have to guess!
  • If the numbers you find are over the target score, you loose and have another chance!
  • Win or loose, your current score will be set to 0 and you'll receive another target score for a chance to add another point!
    • BONUS: Challenge yourself to unlock a new crystal by winning 2 times!
