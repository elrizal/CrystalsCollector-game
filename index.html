Crystal Collector

How does this work?

  1. Check your target score. The numbers you need will need to match this number for a point. This will change each round.
  2. Tap any of the four crystals. Each has a hidden value, so you'll have to guess!
  3. If the numbers you find are over the target score, you loose and have another chance!
  4. Win or loose, your current score will be set to 0 and you'll receive another target score for a chance to add another point!
BONUS: Challenge yourself to unlock a new crystal by winning 5 times!
